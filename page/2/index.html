YY的GCD 题解

gcd(x,y)=k(k∈prime)gcd(x,y) = k(k \in prime)gcd(x,y)=k(k∈prime)

gcd(x/k,y/k)=1gcd(x/k,y/k) = 1gcd(x/k,y/k)=1

正如某位大佬所说，此时应该直接上反演结论：
